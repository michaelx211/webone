Laylah Ali is contemporary visual artist. She wanted to be a lawyer or doctor when she was a child. She in her education received her bachelors of art in english and studio art from Williams college. She completed her independent studies program at the Whitney Museum of American Art. She did a residency at Skowhegan School of Painting and Sculpture. She then got her Master of the Fine Arts in Washington university in Saint Louis and now work as a professor in Williamstown, Massachusetts.
